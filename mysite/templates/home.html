Who We Are

We are a dedicated team of individuals driven by our passion to devise an enjoyable solution that encourages and enhances recycling efforts.

Our Mission

Our mission is to develop an engaging game aimed at raising awareness of littering among students on campus. We aspire to address the issue of littering in a manner that is both enjoyable and environmentally sustainable. To realise this vision, our game incorporates an innovative approach – users can scan QR codes located on bins, to give themselves coins they can play with. Additionally, they can answer a quiz on sustainability for extra coins if they answer correctly. To further promote sustainability, players can compete for the best high score, encouraging them to think more about sustainable waste disposal.

FAQ

How do I earn coins?

You can earn coins by scanning QR codes on bins around campus. You get a base amount from that, and you also have the option to take a sustainability quiz to earn additional coins.

What can I spend my coins on?

You can spend your coins on the Sustainability Slide game, try and get a new highscore to compare with friends.

Is there a limit on scanning QR codes?

You can only scan the same QR code once a day. However, there are multiple QR codes around campus, so there is no limit on how many different QR codes you scan a day!

How does this help the environment?

Our aim with this game is to make people more aware of locations of bins around campus. By enhancing this awareness, we aim to reduce instances of littering and promote a cleaner, more environmentally conscious campus community. Incorporating a quiz component into the game serves as a clever mechanism to encourage ongoing engagement with sustainable practices beyond just waste disposal.
